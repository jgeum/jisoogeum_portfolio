Sentbe

Sentbe is a Korean fin-tech company specializing in oversees remittance. The main service is provided through a mobile application and a website, and their function is to allow users working in South Korea to easily send money overseas with lower transfer rates, service fees, etc. The service is done through a few simple clicks and a verification process that users only have to go through once.

During my time here, I worked on several projects:
1. Designing a promotional website for an oversees remittance mobile app.
2. Conducting competitor research across 20+ international remittance companies for annual report.
3. Designing a concept app based on the competitor research
